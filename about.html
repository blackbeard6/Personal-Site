About Me

I am a senior at Dartmouth College where I am studying computer science. I grew up in Aurora, Ontario and Lawrenceville, New Jersey with my two sisters and my dog, Norman. I love sports, the outdoors, and traveling. I spent this past winter road tripping across New Zealand with a few of my classmates. On the left, check out some of the pictures my friend Owen took throughout the trip. Outside of the classroom I am a member of the Dartmouth Rugby Football Club. I have experience in mobile development and computer vision, and am comfortable coding in C, Java, Python, Swift. I also am good with Figma and enjoy design. I am eager to continue mastering these skills and learning new ones.

Professional Experience

This past summer I interned on the Compass Team in Walmart's Emerging Tech Org. I built end-to-end product detection functionality on the edge in our iOS SDK. The project utilized a YOLOV8 model for object localization and a MobileViT model for image embedding. I deployed the models in TensorFlow using Swift. In the past I also interned on The Land Accelerator as a Rockefeller Center First-Year Fellow. Over the course of this internship I wrote articles, provided feedback to entrepreneurs, and built a database of investors.
