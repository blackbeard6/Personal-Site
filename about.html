About Me

I'm a senior at Dartmouth College, passionate about computer science and creating innovative solutions. Originally from New Jersey, I share my home with two amazing sisters and my loyal companion, Norman the dog. When I'm not coding, I'm exploring my love for sports, the great outdoors, and traveling. One of my recent adventures took me on an exciting road trip across New Zealand with some fellow classmates. On the left, you can browse through captivating photos taken by my friend Owen during our journey. My coding journey includes experience in mobile development and proficiency in languages such as C, Java, Python, and Swift. Additionally, I enjoy dabbling in design using Figma. My main aspiration is to make cutting-edge AI technology more accessible to users, and I'm excited about the possibilities ahead. Thanks for stopping by my page!
